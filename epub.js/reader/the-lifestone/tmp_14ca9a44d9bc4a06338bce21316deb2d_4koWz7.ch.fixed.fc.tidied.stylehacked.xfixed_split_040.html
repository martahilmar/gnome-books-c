A User Selected : A Life Taken.

Christine, drawn from her safe life into the magic and drama of a battle preparing to re-engage. A battle of good verses evil, of life and death and of a search for power and a seeking of control. Christine faces the Dark Lord Nailstead, assisted by the ancient magician Vindec who has drawn her to his hidden world, along with an elf a dwarf and a wolf, and unknown to her the shade of a long dead king amongst others.

Christine stood before the Lifestone, the sword of power hanging from her belt, the wolf her friend standing beside her ready to accept the gift that destiny declared to be hers...

 

Christine’s tale is another story, one told in the second book of
