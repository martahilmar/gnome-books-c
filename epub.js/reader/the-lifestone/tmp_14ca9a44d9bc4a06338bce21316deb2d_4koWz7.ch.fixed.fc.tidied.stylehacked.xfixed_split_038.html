Epilogue

Centuries pass during which the world of the elves and dwarfs fade from sight, to be replaced by the rising of humanity. Legends existing in the world of the King, became stories told to children or written about as fantasy. The King's name still lives, though many doubt the truth of his existence. Whilst all of the changes occurred the King and his knights slept on safe and protected by the Lifestone.

Vindec lives through these changes, slowly growing old but still holding on and always waiting for the call of the Lifestone. Many times the King and his knights sallied fourth, defending the true and honest, vanquishing the evil and demonic, but these episodes failed to be understood by the rulers known as man. With each battle fought, with each breakout from the abyss, the King grew weaker, until finally he returned to the Olar and failed to make it into the cavern. His body turned to stone and he lay safely hidden in the accompaniment of the very few of his surviving knights.

The King has passed from life into shade, but his entity lived on within the bounds of the Olar! Still as always ready to defend the weak, and drive evil from his realm.

The Lifestone took account of the situation, it made arrangements for the future, it sought out humans to become the Users of the King's power, the vessels which could carry the entity of the true King out to battle against the dark forces, it maintained the prophesies that had seen its creation.

The End.
